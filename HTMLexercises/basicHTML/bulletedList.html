Bulleted List

Here is some HTML that creates a bulleted list with 2 bullet points.

<ul>
    <li>Here is the first bullet point</li>
    <li>Here is the second bullet point</li>
</ul>

This creates the list

  • Here is the first bullet point
  • Here is the second bullet point

The ‘ul’ tag declares the starting and ending of an unordered list.
The ‘li’ tag creates a bullet point.

As an exercise, try to create a unordered list of your favorite hobbies.
