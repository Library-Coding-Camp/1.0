Head and Body

The head and body are sections of your website. The head is everything in the head tag, which (usually) includes your javascript imports, and title tag, among other things. The body tag contains the actual content of the webpage - anything that will be displayed to the screen. The head in body tag in action look like this:

<head>
    <!--- add imports and title tag -->
</head>
<body>
    <!--- add h1 tag, paragraph tags, etc.. -->
</body>
<h1 id = “title”></h1>

Note that the id name (id = “idName”) cannot have spaces.
As an exercise, create a cat (or dog) website that uses the head and body tags.
